Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to your questions about Dressify AI.

What is Dressify AI?

Dressify AI is an artificial intelligence-powered fashion platform that provides personalized style recommendations, virtual avatar customization, and professional fashion advice through conversational AI.

How does the AI Recommend outfits?

Our machine learning models analyze your body shape, skin tone, current trends, and personal preferences to suggest outfits from our curated fashion database.

Is the Virtual Avatar Creator free?

Yes, the basic Virtual Avatar Creator is free to use for all registered users. You can create a high-fidelity 3D representation of yourself and try on various styles.

How do I use the Fashion Chat?

Simply navigate to the Chat page and ask our AI assistant anything about fashion, trends, or specific styling advice. It's available 24/7!

Can I download my avatar?

Yes, you can export and download your customized avatars in high resolution to share with friends or save for later inspiration.
